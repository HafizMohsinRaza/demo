The operational principle of Gaztron’s Nitrogen Gas Generator Plant (CU-DX Model)

At Gaztron, we take immense pride in presenting our cutting-edge Nitrogen Gas Generator Plant - the CU-DX Model. This state-of-the-art system operates on a highly advanced and efficient principle, setting new standards in the industry.

The primary goal of our CU-DX Model is to achieve an impressive purity level of 99.9% for Nitrogen gas - a feat accomplished flawlessly by our Standard Model of Nitrogen Gas Generator. This exceptional level of purity ensures that your applications and processes receive the highest-quality Nitrogen, free from impurities that might hinder their performance.

Here's where the magic happens: The Nitrogen, already boasting an exceptional 99.9% purity, undergoes a meticulous purification process. It is meticulously passed through a specially designed tower filled with a top-grade Copper catalyst, while being subjected to a precisely controlled temperature of 180 degrees Celsius. This ingenious process works wonders, reducing the oxygen content from a mere 0.1% to virtually negligible PPM levels.

The result? A nitrogen stream of unparalleled purity, meeting the most stringent industry requirements and exceeding your expectations. Our CU-DX Model stands as a testament to Gaztron's unwavering commitment to delivering the most efficient and reliable Nitrogen Gas Generator Plant on the market.

At Gaztron, we go above and beyond to ensure that our Nitrogen Gas Generator Plant operates at peak efficiency. Our cutting-edge Changeover and Regeneration process are designed to optimize productivity and deliver an unmatched level of purity in the final product.

The Changeover process is seamlessly integrated into the system, occurring after every 4-hour cycle. During this phase, the running tower transitions into the regeneration phase while the regenerated tower takes over the production of pure Nitrogen. This seamless transition ensures uninterrupted Nitrogen supply, maximizing productivity and minimizing downtime.

The magic of our Regeneration process lies in its ability to produce pure Copper, a crucial component for the Nitrogen generation process. To achieve this, pure Nitrogen is passed through a Copper Oxide tower at a precisely controlled temperature of 175 degrees Celsius, along with hydrogen. The resulting chemical reaction yields Pure Copper, as shown in the following equation:

Cu + H2 → Cu+H2O

As a result of this ingenious reaction, the Nitrogen attains an extraordinary purity level of 99.9998%, representing a mere 2PPm of impurities. We take immense pride in consistently delivering Nitrogen of such exceptional quality, surpassing industry standards and meeting the most demanding requirements.

However, we understand that in certain reactions, water moisture can be generated as a byproduct, presenting a potential challenge. But fear not! Our solution involves passing the Gas through our state-of-the-art Gas Dryer, expertly designed to remove any traces of moisture and achieve the desired Dew Point.
