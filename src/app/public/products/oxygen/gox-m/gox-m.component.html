The operational principle of Gaztron’s Oxygen Generator (GOX-M Model)

Discover the power of Gaztron's Oxygen Generator (GOX-M Model), India's undisputed leader in manufacturing top-of-the-line Medical Oxygen Generators for the healthcare sector.

Our revolutionary GOX-M Model operates on a cutting-edge principle that sets it apart from the competition. By intelligently utilizing an adsorbent bed of zeolite, carefully engineered to attract nitrogen more intensely than oxygen, our Oxygen Generator excels at producing highly enriched oxygen from a gas mixture like air.

Under high pressure, the gas mixture flows through the zeolite-filled vessel, where nitrogen is effectively retained in the bed, leaving behind a remarkably oxygen-rich gas. The result? A continuous and reliable supply of high-purity oxygen, tailored to meet the stringent demands of the healthcare industry.

Our GOX-M Model stands head and shoulders above the rest, not only in its superior performance but also in its efficient nitrogen adsorption and regeneration process. When the zeolite bed reaches its nitrogen adsorption capacity, a simple pressure reduction triggers the release of adsorbed nitrogen, seamlessly preparing the system for the next cycle of producing oxygen-enriched air.
